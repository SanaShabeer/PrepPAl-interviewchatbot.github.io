Chatbot Instructions

Welcome to the Virtual Job Interview!

Here's how you can use the chatbot:

  1. Start the Interview: Click on the 'Start Interview' button to begin.
  2. Answer Questions: Provide your responses to the questions asked by the chatbot.
  3. Receive Feedback: After each answer, the chatbot will provide feedback and a similarity score.
  4. Review and Improve: Use the feedback to improve your answers.
